Health Tips to be Followed

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to stay hydrated.
  • Wash your hands regularly with soap to prevent infections.
  • Exercise at least 30 minutes every day to boost your immunity.
  • Eat more fruits and vegetables for a balanced diet.
  • Get regular checkups to detect diseases early.
  • Sleep 7–8 hours daily to allow your body to recover.
  • Maintain mental wellness by practicing relaxation techniques or mindfulness.
  • Avoid smoking, excessive alcohol, and other harmful substances.
  • Keep your environment clean and hygienic to prevent diseases.
  • Follow vaccination schedules and recommended preventive measures.
